About Us

WE DO RAINWATER HARVESTING TO AVOID WATER PROBLEMS IN PAKISTAN.

Fresh water scarcity is disrupting populations, economies, the environment every aspect of life as we know it. The effects are profound in health, food and energy and to the very planet itself. We are witnessing a new crisis threshold, as major cities face total water shutdowns.

There are better ways to manage the water needs of people and planet and for over a decade, Harvest Rain has informed their discovery. It's trusted reporting has enhanced climate agreements, urban policy and citizen activity.

Storing rainwater helps in recharging the aquifers. It helps in preventing urban flooding due to excess rain. The stored water can be used for irrigation practices in farming region. The water can be used for daily use and help in reducing water bills in the towns and cities.

We also want you to do the rain water harvesting at your home, office, etc. It's benifitiall not only for you but for your Country as well. Our mission is to normalize rain harvesting all over Pakistan because no water no life

If you don't know how to set or install rainwater harvesting system at your home. Our team love to help you. Our team come to your place and install proper rain harvesting system for you. Contact Us for more details we'll be in touch with you as soon as possible.

Rainwater Harvesting

Rainwater harvesting or collecting system is the technology that collects and stores rainwater for human use. Rain Water Harvesting provides the best possible alternative and supplementary source of water in a situation where existing water sources are depleting and fail to fulfill the needs of a growing population.
